This guide will walk you through all patient features in Med Agenda, from account creation to managing your appointments and viewing your medical history.

## Getting Started

### Registering a New Account

To create your patient account in Med Agenda:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Patient Registration">
    Access the registration page at `/patient/register`
    
    You'll see the MedAgenda logo and a form titled "Registro de Paciente" (Patient Registration).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in Your Personal Information">
    Complete all required fields in the registration form:
    
    - **Name**: Your full name
    - **CPF**: Your CPF number (digits only, no formatting)
    - **Date of Birth**: Select your birth date from the calendar picker
    - **Address**: Your street address (optional)
    - **Medical History**: Any relevant medical conditions, allergies, or medications (optional)
    - **Email**: Your email address (required for login and notifications)
    - **Password**: Create a secure password
    
    <Note>
      The CPF field only accepts numbers. The system will automatically remove any formatting characters you enter.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Registration">
    Click the **"Registrar Paciente"** (Register Patient) button to create your account.
    
    If successful, you'll be redirected to the patient login page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Logging In

Once you have an account, you can log in to access your patient dashboard:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Login Page">
    Navigate to `/patient/login`
    
    You'll see "Você está acessando como Paciente" (You are accessing as Patient).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Credentials">
    Provide your login information:
    
    - **Email**: The email address you registered with
    - **Password**: Your account password
    
    <Note>
      You can click the eye icon next to the password field to toggle password visibility.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Optional: Remember Me">
    Check the **"Lembrar-me"** (Remember Me) checkbox if you want the system to keep you logged in.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Click the **"Login"** button to access your dashboard.
    
    Upon successful login, you'll be redirected to `/patient/dash`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Don't have an account yet? Click the **"Ainda não tem conta? Registrar"** (Don't have an account? Register) link on the login page to create one.
</Info>

## Finding and Scheduling Appointments

### Searching for Doctors by Specialty

Med Agenda allows you to find doctors based on their specialty:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Using the Search Feature">
    The system maintains a list of doctors with their specialties. When scheduling an appointment, you can:
    
    1. View all available doctors in the dropdown menu
    2. See each doctor's name and CRM number
    3. Review their specialty information before selecting
    
    Common specialties available include:
    - Cardiology
    - Dermatology
    - Pediatrics
    - Orthopedics
    - General Practice
    - And many more
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Doctor Information">
    Each doctor listing shows:
    - **Name**: The doctor's full name
    - **CRM**: Their medical registration number
    - **Specialty**: Their area of medical expertise
    - **Contact**: Telephone number for inquiries
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Scheduling an Appointment

To book a new consultation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Scheduling Page">
    Navigate to the **"Agendar Nova Consulta"** (Schedule New Appointment) section from your patient dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Date and Time">
    Choose when you'd like your appointment:
    
    - Click the **"Data e Hora da Consulta"** (Consultation Date and Time) field
    - Select your preferred date and time using the datetime picker
    
    <Warning>
      Make sure to select a future date and time. Past dates will not be accepted.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Doctor">
    Select a doctor from the dropdown menu:
    
    - Browse the list of available doctors
    - Each entry shows the doctor's name and CRM number
    - Select the doctor you wish to see
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Mark as Urgent (Optional)">
    If your consultation is urgent, check the **"Consulta Urgente"** (Urgent Consultation) checkbox.
    
    This helps prioritize your appointment.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Observations (Optional)">
    Use the **"Observações"** (Observations) field to:
    
    - Describe your symptoms
    - Mention specific concerns
    - Provide any relevant context for your visit
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Your Appointment">
    Click the **"Agendar Consulta"** (Schedule Appointment) button.
    
    You'll see a success message: "Consulta agendada com sucesso!" (Appointment scheduled successfully!)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You must be logged in to schedule appointments. The system uses your stored CPF and email to create the appointment.
</Note>

## Managing Your Appointments

### Viewing Your Dashboard

Your patient dashboard (`/patient/dash`) displays:

- **Total Consultations**: A count of all your scheduled and past appointments
- Quick statistics about your healthcare journey
- Easy navigation to schedule new appointments or view history

### Viewing Appointment History

To review your past consultations:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access History Page">
    Navigate to the **"Histórico Médico"** (Medical History) section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Past Appointments">
    Your history shows all consultations that occurred before today, including:
    
    - **Date**: When the consultation took place (formatted as DD/MM/YYYY)
    - **Doctor Name**: The attending physician
    - **Specialty**: The doctor's medical specialty
    - **Contact**: The doctor's telephone number
    - **Observations**: Any notes from the appointment
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  If you haven't had any appointments yet, you'll see the message: "Você ainda não tem histórico de consultas" (You don't have any consultation history yet).
</Info>

## Email Notifications

Med Agenda keeps you informed through automatic email notifications:

### Appointment Confirmation

When you schedule a new appointment, the system:

1. **Sends a confirmation email** to the address associated with your account
2. **Includes appointment details**:
   - Date and time of your consultation
   - Doctor's name and specialty
   - Clinic location (if applicable)
   - Any special instructions

<Note>
  Make sure your email address is correct in your profile to receive important notifications about your healthcare.
</Note>

### Email Best Practices

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Check Your Inbox">
    - Always check your email after scheduling an appointment
    - Add the Med Agenda email address to your contacts to prevent messages from going to spam
    - Keep confirmation emails for your records
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Update Contact Information">
    If you need to update your email address:
    
    - Contact your clinic administrator
    - Provide your CPF and new email address
    - Verify the change by checking for a test email
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't log in to my account">
    **Possible solutions:**
    
    - Double-check that you're using the correct email address
    - Verify your password (remember it's case-sensitive)
    - Try clicking the eye icon to see your password as you type
    - If you've forgotten your password, contact the clinic administrator to reset it
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I don't see any doctors when trying to schedule">
    **Possible causes:**
    
    - The clinic may not have added doctors to the system yet
    - There may be a temporary system issue
    - Contact your clinic administrator for assistance
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="My appointment isn't showing in my history">
    **Check the following:**
    
    - History only shows past appointments (before today)
    - Future appointments won't appear in history
    - Make sure the appointment date has passed
    - Verify you're logged in with the correct account
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I didn't receive a confirmation email">
    **Steps to resolve:**
    
    - Check your spam/junk folder
    - Verify your email address is correct in your profile
    - Wait a few minutes - emails may be delayed
    - Contact the clinic to confirm your appointment was created
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  For urgent medical issues, always call your clinic directly or seek emergency medical attention. Do not rely solely on the online system for urgent care.
</Warning>
