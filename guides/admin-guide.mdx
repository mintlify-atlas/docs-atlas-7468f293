## Getting Started as an Administrator

### Admin Account Setup

To create your administrator account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Admin Registration">
    Access the registration page at `/admin/register`
    
    You'll see the MedAgenda logo and "Registro de Administrador" (Administrator Registration).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in Your Information">
    Complete the registration form with:
    
    - **Name**: Your full name
    - **Email**: Your administrative email address (used for login)
    - **Password**: Create a secure password
    
    <Warning>
      Use a strong password with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. As an administrator, you have access to sensitive patient and doctor information.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Registration">
    Click **"Registrar Administrador"** (Register Administrator) to create your account.
    
    Upon success, you'll be redirected to `/admin/login`.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Login to Your Account">
    At the login page:
    
    - Enter your registered email
    - Enter your password
    - Optionally check "Lembrar-me" (Remember Me)
    - Click **"Login"** to access your admin dashboard at `/admin/dash`
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Unlike doctors and patients, administrators can self-register. However, in production environments, you may want to restrict this to authorized personnel only.
</Note>

## Managing Doctors

As an administrator, you have full control over doctor accounts in the system.

### Adding a New Doctor

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Doctor Management">
    Navigate to **"Gerenciamento de Médicos"** (Doctor Management) from your admin dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete the Doctor Registration Form">
    Under **"Cadastrar Novo Médico"** (Register New Doctor), fill in all required fields:
    
    **Personal Information:**
    - **Name**: Doctor's full legal name
    - **CRM**: Medical registration number (unique identifier)
    - **Email**: Professional email for login
    - **Password**: Initial password (doctor should change after first login)
    
    **Professional Details:**
    - **Specialty**: Select from the dropdown menu
    - **Telephone**: Contact number
    - **Consultation Value**: Fee in Brazilian Reais (R$)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Medical Specialty">
    Choose from available specialties such as:
    
    - Cardiology (Cardiologia)
    - Dermatology (Dermatologia)
    - Pediatrics (Pediatria)
    - Orthopedics (Ortopedia)
    - General Practice (Clínico Geral)
    - And many more...
    
    <Info>
      Specialties are loaded from `/doctor/specialties.json`. Contact your technical team to add new specialties.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Doctor Registration">
    Click **"Cadastrar Médico"** (Register Doctor).
    
    You'll see a confirmation message: "Médico cadastrado com sucesso!" (Doctor registered successfully!)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Searching for Doctors

To find and verify doctor information:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Search by CRM">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter CRM Number">
        In the **"Buscar Médico por CRM"** (Search Doctor by CRM) section:
        
        - Type the doctor's CRM number
        - Click **"Buscar"** (Search)
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="View Doctor Details">
        The system displays:
        
        - CRM number
        - Full name
        - Email address
        - Specialty
        - Telephone
        - Consultation value
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Search by Other Criteria">
    You can also search doctors using the API endpoints:
    
    - **By Name**: `GET /doctor/search?name={name}`
    - **By Specialty**: `GET /doctor/search?specialty={specialty}`
    - **By Email**: `GET /doctor/search?email={email}`
    - **List All**: `GET /doctor`
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Updating Doctor Information

To modify a doctor's profile:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the Doctor">
    First, search for the doctor by CRM to verify their current information.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update via API">
    Use the endpoint: `PUT /doctor/{crm}`
    
    You can update:
    - Name
    - Email
    - Password
    - Specialty
    - Telephone
    - Consultation value
    
    <Note>
      The CRM number cannot be changed as it's the unique identifier.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Changes">
    Search for the doctor again to confirm the updates were applied correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Removing Doctors

To remove a doctor from the system:

<Warning>
  Deleting a doctor may affect historical consultation records. Ensure you have proper backups and understand the implications before proceeding.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Doctor CRM">
    Confirm you have the correct CRM number for the doctor you want to remove.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Delete Doctor">
    Use the endpoint: `DELETE /doctor/{crm}`
    
    This permanently removes the doctor from the system.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Verify the doctor no longer appears in searches or doctor lists.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Patients

Administrators can create, view, update, and manage patient accounts.

### Registering a New Patient

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Patient Management">
    Navigate to **"Gerenciamento de Pacientes"** (Patient Management) from your admin dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Out Patient Form">
    Under **"Cadastrar Novo Paciente"** (Register New Patient), enter:
    
    **Required Fields:**
    - **Name**: Patient's full name
    - **CPF**: Patient's CPF number (numbers only)
    - **Date of Birth**: Select from date picker
    - **Email**: Patient's email address
    - **Password**: Initial password for patient login
    
    **Optional Fields:**
    - **Address**: Residential address
    - **Medical History**: Existing conditions, allergies, medications
    
    <Tip>
      The CPF field automatically removes any formatting characters. Just type the numbers.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Registration">
    Click **"Cadastrar Paciente"** (Register Patient).
    
    Success message: "Paciente cadastrado com sucesso!" (Patient registered successfully!)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Searching for Patients

To find patient information:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search by CPF">
    In the **"Buscar Paciente por CPF"** section:
    
    - Enter the patient's CPF number
    - Click **"Buscar"** (Search)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Patient Details">
    The system displays:
    
    - CPF
    - Full name
    - Email
    - Date of birth
    - Address
    - Medical history
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Individual Search">
    Use the search form to find one patient at a time by CPF.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="List All Patients">
    Use the API endpoint `GET /patients/list` to retrieve all patients in the system.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Updating Patient Information

To modify patient records:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Patient">
    Search for the patient by CPF to confirm their current information.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Patient Data">
    Use the endpoint: `PUT /patients/update/{cpf}`
    
    You can update:
    - Name
    - Email
    - Password
    - Date of birth
    - Address
    - Medical history
    
    <Warning>
      The CPF cannot be changed as it's the unique identifier for patients.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Changes">
    Search for the patient again to ensure updates were saved correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Removing Patients

To delete a patient account:

<Warning>
  Deleting a patient will affect their consultation history and medical records. Exercise extreme caution and ensure proper data retention policies are followed.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm Patient CPF">
    Verify you have the correct CPF for the patient to be removed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Delete Patient">
    Use the endpoint: `DELETE /patients/delete/{cpf}`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Deletion">
    Confirm the patient no longer appears in system searches.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Consultations

As an administrator, you have full oversight of all consultations in the system.

### Creating Consultations

Administrators can schedule appointments on behalf of patients:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Consultation Management">
    Navigate to **"Gerenciamento de Consultas"** (Consultation Management).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Consultation Details">
    Under **"Agendar Nova Consulta"** (Schedule New Consultation):
    
    - **Date and Time**: Select using the datetime picker
    - **Patient CPF**: Enter the patient's CPF
    - **Doctor CRM**: Enter the doctor's CRM
    - **Urgent**: Check if this is an urgent consultation
    - **Observations**: Add any relevant notes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Consultation">
    Click **"Agendar Consulta"** (Schedule Consultation).
    
    The system will:
    - Create the consultation record
    - Send email notification to the patient
    - Add appointment to doctor's agenda
    
    <Info>
      Email notifications are sent automatically when consultations are created.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Viewing All Consultations

To see all consultations in the system:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="List All Consultations">
    Use the endpoint: `GET /consultations/all`
    
    This returns every consultation with:
    - Consultation ID
    - Date and time
    - Patient CPF
    - Doctor CRM
    - Urgent status
    - Observations
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Search Specific Consultation">
    To find a specific appointment:
    
    1. Get the consultation ID
    2. Use `GET /consultations/{id}`
    3. View complete details
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Patient History">
    View all consultations for a specific patient:
    
    Use: `GET /consultations/patient-history/{cpf}`
    
    Shows all past and upcoming appointments for that patient.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Searching for Consultations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Consultation ID">
    In the **"Buscar Consulta por ID"** (Search Consultation by ID) section:
    
    - Type the consultation ID
    - Click **"Buscar"** (Search)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Consultation Details">
    The system displays:
    
    - Consultation ID
    - Date and time (formatted for pt-BR locale)
    - Patient CPF
    - Doctor CRM
    - Urgent status (Yes/No)
    - Observations
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Updating Consultations

To modify an existing consultation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Consultation ID">
    First, search for the consultation to get its ID.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Consultation">
    Use the endpoint: `PUT /consultations/update`
    
    You can change:
    - Date and time
    - Doctor assignment
    - Urgent status
    - Observations
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Notify Affected Parties">
    If you change the date/time or doctor:
    - Inform the patient
    - Notify the doctor(s) involved
    - Update any related records
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Canceling Consultations

To cancel an appointment:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Consultation">
    Obtain the consultation ID for the appointment to cancel.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Delete Consultation">
    Use the endpoint: `DELETE /consultations/{id}`
    
    This removes the consultation from the system.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Communicate Cancellation">
    - Notify the patient
    - Inform the doctor
    - Offer to reschedule if appropriate
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Administrative Workflows

### Onboarding a New Doctor

<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather Information">
    Collect:
    - Full name and CRM
    - Email and phone
    - Specialty
    - Consultation fees
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Account">
    Register the doctor in the system with all details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide Credentials">
    Share login credentials securely:
    - Email address (username)
    - Initial password
    - Login URL: `/doctor/login`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Setup">
    Confirm:
    - Doctor can log in successfully
    - Profile information is correct
    - They appear in search results
    - Patients can select them for appointments
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Handling Patient Requests

<Steps>
  <Step title="Registration Assistance">
    When patients need help registering:
    - Create their account as an admin
    - Verify all information is accurate
    - Provide login credentials securely
    - Guide them through first login
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Account Issues">
    For login or access problems:
    - Verify account exists (search by CPF)
    - Reset password if needed
    - Update email if changed
    - Test login after changes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Scheduling Support">
    When patients need appointment help:
    - Create consultation on their behalf
    - Verify doctor availability
    - Confirm email notification sent
    - Provide consultation ID for reference
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Conflicts

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Double Booking">
    If a doctor is double-booked:
    
    1. Identify the conflicting consultations
    2. Contact both patients
    3. Reschedule one appointment
    4. Update the consultation record
    5. Send new confirmation emails
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Doctor Unavailability">
    When a doctor becomes unavailable:
    
    1. List all their upcoming consultations
    2. Contact affected patients
    3. Offer alternative doctors or times
    4. Update or cancel consultations
    5. Document the changes
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Patient Cancellations">
    When patients cancel:
    
    1. Get the consultation ID
    2. Delete the consultation
    3. Notify the doctor
    4. Update any related records
    5. Offer to reschedule if appropriate
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## API Endpoints Reference

Key endpoints for administrators:

### Admin Endpoints
| Endpoint | Method | Purpose |
|----------|--------|----------|
| `/admin/create` | POST | Create admin account |
| `/admin/login` | POST | Admin authentication |
| `/admin/{id}` | GET | View admin details |
| `/admin/{id}` | DELETE | Remove admin |

### Doctor Management
| Endpoint | Method | Purpose |
|----------|--------|----------|
| `/doctor/create` | POST | Register new doctor |
| `/doctor/{crm}` | PUT | Update doctor info |
| `/doctor/{crm}` | DELETE | Remove doctor |
| `/doctor` | GET | List all doctors |
| `/doctor/search` | GET | Search by CRM/name/specialty/email |

### Patient Management
| Endpoint | Method | Purpose |
|----------|--------|----------|
| `/patients/create` | POST | Register new patient |
| `/patients/{cpf}` | GET | View patient details |
| `/patients/list` | GET | List all patients |
| `/patients/update/{cpf}` | PUT | Update patient info |
| `/patients/delete/{cpf}` | DELETE | Remove patient |

### Consultation Management
| Endpoint | Method | Purpose |
|----------|--------|----------|
| `/consultations/create` | POST | Schedule consultation |
| `/consultations/{id}` | GET | View consultation |
| `/consultations/all` | GET | List all consultations |
| `/consultations/update` | PUT | Modify consultation |
| `/consultations/{id}` | DELETE | Cancel consultation |
| `/consultations/patient-history/{cpf}` | GET | Patient's consultations |

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Email notifications aren't being sent">
    **Check:**
    - Email server configuration
    - Patient email addresses are correct
    - Spam filters aren't blocking messages
    - Consultation creation is successful
    - Network connectivity
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Cannot create doctor with specific specialty">
    **Solution:**
    - Verify specialty exists in `/doctor/specialties.json`
    - Check spelling matches exactly
    - Contact technical team to add new specialties
    - Try selecting from dropdown instead of typing
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Patient can't log in after registration">
    **Verify:**
    - Account was created successfully
    - Email address is correct
    - Password meets requirements
    - Patient is using `/patient/login` (not admin or doctor login)
    - Try resetting the password
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Consultation doesn't appear in doctor's agenda">
    **Check:**
    - CRM number is correct
    - Consultation was created successfully
    - Doctor is logged in with correct account
    - Refresh the doctor's agenda page
    - Verify consultation date is in the future
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
